Lenox Blessed

Dancer | Choreographer | Digital Content Creator | Performer

Biography

Lenox Blessed is a dynamic and versatile artist known for his unique style, creativity, and captivating stage presence. His journey has seen him grace stages with renowned dance crews like House of Swat, Young Impact, Young TIT, and Kimya Kimya, leaving an indelible mark on the Kenyan dance scene.

Experience & Achievements

  • 1st Place, Dance Afrique Season 2 (Solo & Duo)
  • Choreographer for Tanasha Donna's music video
  • Led the viral "Bazokizo" dance challenge
